# Risk Calculation Model

## Purpose
Determine operational risk based on knowledge concentration within areas.

## Risk Factors
- Number of employees per area
- Number of employees with critical knowledge
- Proficiency levels
- Employee status (leaving increases risk)

## Risk Levels
- Low: Knowledge distributed across multiple employees
- Medium: Knowledge concentrated in few employees
- High: Knowledge held by one or leaving employee

## Notes
This model is rule-based and does not use machine learning.
